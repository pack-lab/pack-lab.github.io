Five students from the PACK Lab, Anthony Martinez, Keaton Safu, Jordan D. Strand, Anastasia Tkachuk, and Thanh T. Truong, presented their work at the 3rd MAE Research Symposium. 

* Poster 1: LEGO Manual Instruction Parsing via ML models & Legality Models Structured Assembly State Export, by Anthony Martinez
* Poster 2: Truck-Drone Routing with Dynamics-Informed Energy Constraints for Last-Mile Deliveries, by Keaton Safu
* Poster 3: A Trust-Based Decision Framework for Autonomous Vehicle Control in Mixed-Autonomy Traffic, by Jordan D. Strand
* Poster 4: On the Efficacy of Centralized Training and Decentralized Execution for DQN-Based Traffic Control, by Anastasia Tkachuk
* Poster 5: AMixed-Dimensional Path Planning for Multi-Agent Frontier Exploration, by Thanh T. Truong

Great work, team!
